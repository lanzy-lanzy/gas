# Cashier Reports - Quick Start Guide

## Access the Reports

### From Sidebar
1. Click on **Reports** in left sidebar
2. Click on **Cashier Reports** (new link added)
3. Select report type: Daily, Monthly, or Yearly

### Direct Links
- **Daily:** `/dealer/cashiers/reports/daily/`
- **Monthly:** `/dealer/cashiers/reports/monthly/`
- **Yearly:** `/dealer/cashiers/reports/yearly/`

---

## Report Types

### Daily Report
**Shows:** Individual day's performance

**Date Selection:**
- Pick specific date from calendar
- Defaults to today

**Metrics:**
- Income per cashier
- Products delivered that day
- Daily totals

**Use Case:** Track daily operations, see who processed what orders

---

### Monthly Report
**Shows:** Entire month's performance

**Period Selection:**
- Select Year (e.g., 2025)
- Select Month (Jan-Dec dropdown)
- Defaults to current month

**Metrics:**
- Monthly cashier income
- Monthly product movement
- Monthly totals

**Use Case:** Analyze monthly trends, track seasonal patterns

---

### Yearly Report
**Shows:** Full year performance with monthly breakdown

**Period Selection:**
- Select Year (e.g., 2025)
- Defaults to current year

**Metrics:**
- Annual cashier income
- Annual product distribution
- **Special:** Monthly breakdown cards showing Jan-Dec performance

**Use Case:** Annual review, plan next year's strategy

---

## Understanding the Data

### Top Summary Cards
| Card | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Total Income | Sum of all delivered orders in period |
| Total Orders | Count of orders processed |
| Total Units | Products delivered (quantity) |

### Income Table (Left Side)
Shows which cashier generated the most revenue.

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Cashier | Staff name + Employee ID |
| Orders | How many orders they processed |
| Total | Revenue they generated (₱) |
| Avg | Average sale amount |

**Sorted by:** Highest income first

### Inventory Table (Right Side)
Shows which products were delivered the most.

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Product | Product name + size |
| Qty | Units delivered |
| Revenue | Total sales for this product (₱) |
| Price/Unit | Average unit price |

**Sorted by:** Most units first

### Monthly Breakdown (Yearly Only)
Grid showing each month's performance.

**Each Card Shows:**
- Month name
- Income (₱)
- Units delivered
- Number of orders

## Sample Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Check Today's Sales
1. Open Cashier Reports
2. Stay on "Daily Report" tab
3. Check "Total Income" card
4. Review "Income by Cashier" table to see who performed best

### Scenario 2: Track November Performance
1. Open Cashier Reports
2. Click "Monthly Report" tab
3. Select Year: 2025, Month: November
4. View total income and product mix
5. See which cashiers performed best

### Scenario 3: Annual Review
1. Open Cashier Reports
2. Click "Yearly Report" tab
3. Select Year: 2025
4. Review top section for annual totals
5. Scroll down to see monthly breakdown cards
6. Identify seasonal trends (which months were strongest)

## Key Insights to Look For

### Income Insights
- **Best Cashier:** Highest value in "Total Amount" column
- **Consistent Performer:** High "Order Count" with reasonable "Average"
- **Top Month:** Check monthly breakdown for peak season

### Inventory Insights
- **Popular Product:** Highest "Qty" in inventory table
- **High Value Product:** High revenue but lower quantity
- **Growth Product:** Compare months to see trends

### Combined Insights
- **Which cashier sells most of X product?** Not directly shown, but can filter by opening report and noting patterns
- **Sales per unit by product:** Check "Price/Unit" column
- **Productivity:** Orders per day/month

## Tips & Tricks

### Date Selection
- **Daily:** Use calendar to pick dates in past or future
- **Monthly:** Can view any month/year combination
- **Yearly:** Useful for 2024, 2025, 2026 comparisons

### Sorting & Filtering
- Reports auto-sort by highest values
- Income table sorted by total amount
- Inventory table sorted by quantity delivered

### Empty Results
- If no data shows: No orders delivered in that period
- Try different date range
- Check if cashiers have processed any orders

### Color Coding
- **Orange:** Income/Revenue metrics
- **Blue:** Order/count metrics
- **Green:** Inventory/quantity metrics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why is a cashier not showing in the report?**
A: They haven't processed any delivered orders in that period yet.

**Q: Can I compare two months?**
A: Generate monthly reports for each month separately and compare the totals.

**Q: Why is product X showing high revenue but low quantity?**
A: It has a high unit price. Check "Price/Unit" column.

**Q: Can I export these reports?**
A: Currently print-friendly. Use browser print-to-PDF feature.

**Q: How far back can I view reports?**
A: As far back as you have delivered orders in the system.
